About Me

I sit at the intersection of mechanical engineering and venture capital, which means I can read a cap table and a stress-strain curve with equal fluency.

Currently finishing my Master's across UC Berkeley Haas School of Business, EDHEC Business Schoool and Sungkyunkwan GSB. Last summer I screened 150+ MedTech startups at CRB HealthTech Fund III (Barcelona), built valuation models from scratch, and co-authored an LP report covering a €7.5B market. Before that, I ran international acceleration programs at Incubateur HEC Paris for deep-tech founders from India, South Korea, and Japan — brokering commercial conversations with TotalEnergies, L'Oréal, and Thales.

My engineering background isn't decorative. It's why I can interrogate a robotics startup's BOM, challenge an AI company's inference cost assumptions, or evaluate whether a hardware moat is real. I believe the best investment decisions happen when financial rigor meets technical honesty.
